Operations

  • Project Manager

    Management will be of commercial projects within our healthcare core market,. Senior project managers will work closely with all members of the project team, project executives and regional leadership teams and will be responsible for the following:

    • 100% detailed/hands-on knowledge of project scope.

    • Key point of contact with IT department and pharmacies.

    • Challenge & support jobsite and self-perform work team.

    • Accountable for project completion and financials critical success factors, customer satisfaction results.

    Qualifications

    We are looking for a flexible, detail-oriented team player with the ability to manage multiple tasks, produce quality work, and consistently meet deadlines. The successful candidate will possess:

    • Excellent listening skills and strong communication skills.

    • Ability to identify and resolve complex issues.

    • Ability to create and support team morale.

    • Demonstrated understanding of building processes and systems.

    • Work scope requires complete understanding of cost estimating, budgeting and forecasting.

    • Proficient computer skills in Microsoft Office Suite, project management software (Prolog or similar), accounting cost management software (CMIC or similar), and scheduling software (Primavera or similar).

    • Experience with running multiple complex, highly technical projects preferably within DPR's core markets.

    • Bachelor's degree in construction management, engineering or related field.

    • A strong work ethic and a "can-do" attitude.

    • This position is salaried.

Development

  • Developer

    What you'll do:

    • Develop, test and implement IoT software 

    • Develop, test and deploy mobile app (iOS and Android) to interact with IoT sensors

    • Develop software apps that can be used for technical support

    • Build connectivity layer for data access between IoT platform and analytics

    • Work with electronics hardware design team to develop software to test the sensor and IoT devices

    • Interface with peers from IoT team to understand and document software requirements

    • Communicate the project progress regularly

    Qualifications

    What you'll need:

    • Bachelor's degree in engineering (electrical, electronics, or computer science) with a minimum of 10 years of experience with steadily increasing responsibilities as an Engineer, or a master's degree in engineering (electrical, electronics, or computer science) with a minimum of 8 years of experience with steadily increasing responsibilities as an Engineer

    • Hands on experience in SOAP and REST API client server communication using XML and JSON data parsing

    • Experience with the following programming languages HTML5.0, Python, Java, JavaScript, NodeJS

    • Experience in Android, iOS mobile applications and Web-based applications.

    • Knowledge of the TCP/IP Stack

    • Cloud development experience in AWS IoT

    • Knowledge of SQL and No-SQL databases

    • Experience with network protocols TCP/IP, HTTPS, CoAP/LWM2M, MQTT, BLE

    • Strong knowledge of BLE is a plus

    • Knowledge with network security techniques, TLS, Authentication, Public Key Cryptography, Encryption, and Secure Firmware Updates

    • Good understanding and hands on experience with IoT devices and IoT Platforms

    • Hands on experience developing in a Unix/Linux environment

    • Experience with the development and use of IOT related technologies at the Edge: IOT protocols, local and back haul connectivity technologies and industrial sensor technologies
