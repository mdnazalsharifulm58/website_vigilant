About Us.

Using technology to improve the process of care

Our History

Vigilant Labels was founded by Dr. Peter Baek, an anesthesiologist, to improve the process of medication labeling and documentation in hospitals and surgery centers.

He knew that proper medication labeling was vital for optimal patient safety. Through collaboration with Pharmacy experts he came to realize the impact medication labeling compliance and proper documentation practices had on revenue and cost.

His familiarity with other solutions in the market and their inability to support clinical workflow led him to develop the Vigilant system, which is described as the easiest way to accurately label syringe medications in the OR.

Currently Setup for Failure

For two decades, IT solutions have been streamlining laborious multi-step processes. Unfortunately, the process anesthsiologists must execute to label and document administered drugs has been overlooked. Considering the level of education and training, this oversight is quite remarkable. Vigilant has developed the right solution to this niche problem.

Vigilant's solution has generated a payback in less than 3 months for all clients that have fully utilized the solution.
